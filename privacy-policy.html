Privacy Policy

BubbleSort — by CassiGames · Last updated: April 15, 2026

Introduction

BubbleSort is a casual puzzle game developed by CassiGames. We respect your privacy and keep things simple: we collect as little as possible, we don't sell your data, and everything that leaves your device is listed below.

What We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear up front. BubbleSort does not collect:

  • Your name or email address
  • Your location (beyond the country-level region AdMob may infer from your IP)
  • Your contacts, photos, files, microphone, or camera
  • Any data linked to your real-world identity

BubbleSort requests no sensitive permissions from your device.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

BubbleSort shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier, general region (country/city level inferred from IP), and interaction data with the ads themselves. This data is processed by Google under their own privacy policy.

All ads are filtered to the Teen (T) content rating or lower, so no mature themes, violence, or inappropriate content will be shown.

You can read more at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

BubbleSort offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase that disables interruptive ads. Rewarded ads (the ones you choose to watch for in-game rewards) remain available but are always optional.

Consent for Personalized Ads (UMP / GDPR)

In the European Economic Area, the UK, and Switzerland, BubbleSort uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to ask for your consent before showing personalized ads. On first launch (or after your previous choice expires) you'll see Google's consent form explaining what data is used and giving you the choice between:

  • Personalized ads — AdMob and its partners may use your device's advertising identifier and activity to serve ads tailored to your interests.
  • Non-personalized ads — ads are still shown but are not personalized based on your past behaviour.

Your choice is remembered by the UMP SDK on your device. You can change it at any time in Settings → Ad Preferences — this reopens the same Google consent form so you can grant or withdraw consent without reinstalling the app. This satisfies your right to withdraw consent at any time under the GDPR.

Outside the EEA/UK/Switzerland, no consent form is shown because one isn't required, and the "Ad Preferences" button is hidden.

Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

In release builds, BubbleSort uses Firebase Crashlytics to help us find and fix bugs. When the app crashes, Crashlytics sends Google a technical crash report containing:

  • The stack trace of the error
  • Your device model and OS version
  • The app version that crashed

Crash reports do not include any personal information, game content, or account data. Crashlytics is disabled entirely in debug/development builds.

In-App Purchases

BubbleSort offers optional in-app purchases (currently only: "Remove Ads") processed entirely through Google Play. Google handles payment and receives your payment details — CassiGames never sees or stores any card or billing information. We only receive a purchase token from Google confirming the purchase was completed.

Google Play Games Services (Cloud Save)

Signing in with Google Play Games is optional. If you sign in, we use your Google Play Games player ID to sync your game progress (level completion, star ratings, power-up inventory, streak data) to Google's cloud so you can restore it on another device or after reinstalling.

We do not receive your email address, real name, or any other profile information from your Google account. You can disconnect your account at any time through your device's Google Play Games settings.

If you don't sign in, all game progress is stored locally on your device only.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

BubbleSort is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, in line with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Google Sign-In is optional and not required to play. Advertisements are filtered to Teen rating or lower — no mature themes, graphic violence, or inappropriate content will be shown.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. The only third-party services integrated in the app are the Google services described above (AdMob, Firebase Crashlytics, Google Play Billing, Google Play Games Services), each operating under Google's own privacy policy.

Data Storage

Game data (level progress, power-up inventory, settings) is stored locally on your device. If you sign in with Google Play Games, that progress is additionally synced to Google's cloud. Uninstalling the app removes all local data; cloud-saved progress can be restored by signing in again on any device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
