Privacy Policy

BubbleSort — by CassiGames · Last updated: March 22, 2026

Introduction

BubbleSort is a casual puzzle game developed by CassiGames. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our data practices. This policy explains what information is collected when you use BubbleSort.

Information We Collect

BubbleSort offers optional sign-in through Google Play Games. If you choose to sign in, we receive your Google Play Games player ID to save and sync your game progress across devices. We do not access your email address, real name, or any other personal information from your Google account.

If you choose not to sign in, all game progress is stored locally on your device only.

Cloud Save

When signed in with Google Play Games, your game progress (level completion, star ratings, power-up inventory, and streak data) is saved to Google's cloud storage. This allows you to restore your progress if you switch devices or reinstall the app. You can disconnect your account at any time through your device's Google Play Games settings.

Advertising

BubbleSort displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information to serve relevant ads, including your device's advertising identifier, general location (country/region level), and interaction data with ads. This data is collected and processed by Google according to their own privacy policy.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use their services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

BubbleSort offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase that disables all non-voluntary advertisements. Rewarded ads (which you choose to watch for in-game rewards) remain available but are entirely optional.

In-App Purchases

BubbleSort offers a single in-app purchase ("Remove Ads") processed through Google Play. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

BubbleSort is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Google Sign-In is optional and not required to play the game. All advertisements are filtered to the most restrictive content rating (G — General Audience) to ensure they are appropriate for players of all ages, including young children.

Ad Content

All ads displayed in BubbleSort are configured with the maximum child-safety settings available through Google AdMob. Ad content is filtered to General Audience (G) rating, meaning no mature themes, violence, or inappropriate content will be shown. If you encounter an ad you believe is inappropriate, please contact us.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal data with third parties. The only third-party service integrated in the app is Google AdMob for advertising, which operates under Google's privacy policy.

Data Storage

Game data (level progress, power-up inventory, settings) is stored locally on your device. If you sign in with Google Play Games, progress is additionally synced to Google's cloud storage for backup and cross-device access. Uninstalling the app will remove locally stored data, but cloud-saved progress can be restored by signing in again on any device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at info@cassigames.nl.
